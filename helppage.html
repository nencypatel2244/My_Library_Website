Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I search for books?

You can use the search bar on the homepage to find books by title, author, or genre.

How can I borrow a book?

Click on the book you want, then click "Add to Cart". Proceed to checkout to borrow.

What is the borrowing duration?

You can borrow a book for 14 days. Late returns may incur a fine.

How do I return a book?

Log in to your account, go to "My Borrowed Books", and follow the return instructions.
